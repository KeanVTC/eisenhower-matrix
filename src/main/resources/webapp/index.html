“ The Eisenhower Matrix (or Priority Matrix) is a tool used to prioritize tasks based on their level of importance and urgency. It consists of four quadrants and tasks are prioritized based on their placement in these quadrants. ”

Create or log-in to your matrix
  • 📌 Create tasks and prioritize them on the board,
  • 📄 Attach documents to go in greater details about a task,
  • ⚙️ Standalone web app (not a third party plugin or template)
  • 📱 Mobile ready,
  • 👍🏻 Free and open-source project.
